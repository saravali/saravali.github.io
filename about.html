West meets East

Western and Vedic astrology seem to be two different topics. And so most astrology programs support only one of them.

But both are essentially the same, they are two different views of the same thing.

Ancient Knowledge meets Modern Technology

The knowledge of classical scriptures seems far away from IT technology and modern scientifical point of view.

On the other side the world suffers from too much concentration on rational thinking combined with exploitation of human and natural ressources.

So both must come together to make us strong and get an idea of a different kind of progress.
