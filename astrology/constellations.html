28 vs. 27 Nakshatras

Some techniques in Vedic astrology use the 28 Nakshatra zodiac having the star Abhijit between Uttara Shadha and Sravana.

The rule is

  • The last quarter (3°20) of Uttara Shadha and the
  • first 15th part (53'20'') of Sravana belong to Abhijit
